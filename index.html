Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026  ·  Effective: March 17, 2026

MYVVA is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We collect only what's necessary to provide the service and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Who We Are

MYVVA (My Vehicle Virtual Assistant) is an AI-powered vehicle maintenance tracking application developed and operated by Christopher Sprague ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MYVVA mobile application and web platform.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@myvva.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided when you sign in via Google or email
  • Vehicle information — VIN numbers, make, model, year, and mileage you provide
  • Usage data — mileage snapshots, maintenance history, and service records you enter or upload
  • Service invoices — PDF or image files you upload for automatic processing; these are processed and not stored permanently
  • Inspection photos — photos you submit for AI-powered vehicle inspections; these are sent to our AI provider for analysis and immediately discarded
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version for app compatibility

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the MYVVA service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track and calculate your vehicle maintenance schedule
  • To process uploaded service invoices and extract relevant maintenance data
  • To provide AI-powered vehicle inspections and diagnostics
  • To provide cost estimates and service recommendations
  • To improve the accuracy of our maintenance recommendations over time using anonymized, aggregated data
  • To send maintenance reminders (only if you opt in)

4. Third-Party Services

MYVVA uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Auth0 — authentication and identity management. Auth0 Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI processing for vehicle analysis, invoice parsing, and inspection photo assessment. Files and photos are sent to Anthropic for processing and are not retained by Anthropic beyond the immediate request. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • NHTSA vPIC API — VIN decoding. This is a US government service and no personal data is shared.
  • MongoDB Atlas — secure cloud database storage for your vehicle and maintenance data
  • eBay Browse API — parts search and pricing. Search queries may include vehicle year, make, and model but no personal identifying information

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas hosted on Microsoft Azure in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission
  • JWT-based authentication via Auth0 for all API requests
  • Environment variable-based secret management — no credentials stored in code
  • Per-user data isolation — you can only access your own data

6. Invoice and Document Processing

When you upload a service invoice or receipt, the document is sent to the Anthropic Claude API for data extraction and then discarded. We do not permanently store the original document — only the structured data extracted from it (service date, mileage, costs, etc.) is retained in your account.

7. Inspection Photo Processing

When you submit a photo for AI inspection (e.g. tire tread, brake pads, battery), the image is sent directly to the Anthropic Claude API for analysis and is immediately discarded after processing. MYVVA does not store your inspection photos. Only the structured result (pass/monitor/fail, observations, recommendation) is saved to your account.

8. Aggregated Data

We may use anonymized, aggregated data — such as average service costs by region or common maintenance intervals by vehicle type — to improve recommendations across the platform. This data cannot be linked to any individual user and is used solely to make MYVVA more accurate and useful for everyone.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from any communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@myvva.app.

11. Children's Privacy

MYVVA is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify users via the app or email.
